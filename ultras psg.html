Paris Saint-Germain Football Club (PSG) is the most popular football club in France and one of the teams with the widest support in the world. Famous PSG fans include Nicolas Sarkozy, Tony Parker, Fabio Quartararo, Tom Brady, Patrick Dempsey, Victoria Azarenka, Teddy Rainer and DJ Snake. Lacking a large passionate fan base, the club began offering cheaper season tickets to young fans in 1976.
